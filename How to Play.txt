Start: Run the script in your terminal or IDE.
Guess: Type a single letter and press Enter.
Correct Guess: If the letter is in the word, it will appear in its correct position.  
Incorrect Guess: If the letter is not in the word, your "Attempts remaining" will decrease by one.  
Winning: Guess all the letters in the secret word before your attempts run out.  
Losing: If you reach 6 incorrect guesses, the game ends and reveals the secret word.
